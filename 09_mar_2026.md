Silicon Valley, San Francisco, and Los Angeles are key California, USA economic and cultural hubs. Silicon Valley (Santa Clara Valley) is the premier global tech hub, while San Francisco serves as a dense, cultured city hub, and Los Angeles is known for entertainment, arts, and diverse, sunny living.
